Our Vision
"Fight Galactic Unemployment"





"We ask recruiters what makes for a successful resume. Then we take these insights
and build them into our resume templates. Thus, when you use our online resume
builder, you know you are giving recruiters exactly what they want."







Idea

By combining our respective
backgrounds in technology and design,
we three came up with the idea of an
intuitive, web-based tool that would
make it as simple as possible to
produce an eye-catching resume.



Outcome

After a rigorous development and
testing process, the outcome was an
online resume builder that combines
functionality and aesthetics to
maximize users' chances of being
invited for job interviews.

The Team

We have always believed that co-creation with users and recruiters is the key to our success. By soliciting
the feedback of both groups, we ensure that users' and recruiters' needs continually align with our
products. Not only does this allow us to improve the current application, but it has led to several ideas for
additional products and services.
