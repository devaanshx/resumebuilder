What is a resume?

A resume (also known as a CV, or curriculum vitae) is a 1-2 page document that
summarizes your work experience and career history.

It usually includes information about the following:

  • Your work history
  • Educational background
  • Achievements
  • Contact information
  • Resume summary or resume objective

What's the difference between a CV and a resume?

In the EU, the words "CV" and "resume" are used interchangeably.

In the United States, however, a resume is a document you use to apply for jobs,
while a CV is mainly used by academics.

Want to learn more? Check out our article on the differences between CVs and
resumes.
